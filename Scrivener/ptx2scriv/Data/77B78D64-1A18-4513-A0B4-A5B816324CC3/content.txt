<statement>
   <p> Convert from 10.4 psia to mm Hg Vacuum </p>
</statement>
<answer>
   <p>10.4 psia = 222.3 mm Hg (vacuum)</p>
</answer>
<solution>
   <p>
      <ol>
         <li>
            <p> Set up a ratio to convert psia to mm Hg Absolute </p>
            <p>
               <me>\frac{10.4 \textrm{ psia}}{14.7 \textrm{ psia}} = \frac{p}{760 \textrm{ mm Hg}
                    }</me>
               <me>p = 537.7 \textrm{ mm Hg (absolute)}</me>
            </p>
         </li>
         <li>
            <p> Subtract from atmospheric pressure to get vacuum </p>
            <p>
               <me>V = 760 - p = 222.3 \textrm{ mm Hg (vacuum)}</me>
            </p>
         </li>
      </ol>
   </p>
</solution>
