The first of these two turbines is known as the high pressure turbine (H.P.) and the second is the low pressure turbine (L.P.). A steam line, called the crossover carries steam from the outlet of the H.P. turbine to the inlet of the L.P. turbine. At normal operating speeds, the steam leaving the H.P. turbine and entering the crossover is at a pressure below atmospheric.
