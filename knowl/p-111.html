When steam is not available for atomization, such as when lighting off a dead ship, a steam assist atomizer can not be used. Under these circumstances, a straight mechanical atomizer is used instead. In this design, fuel pressure alone atomizes the fuel into tiny particles. The quality of the atomization depends on the fuel pressure, so this design is not particularly suitable for use when the boiler load and fuel pressure are varying frequently, as it requires the operator to frequently change sprayer plates to compensate for varying fuel pressures.
