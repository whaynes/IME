The register assembly consists of three main parts. The movable air doors allow the operator to open and close the register. The doors are opened wide when the register is in service, and closed when it is not being used. The diffuser plate helps the air to mix evenly with the oil and it prevents the flame from being blown back behind the atomizer. The stationary air foils guide the majority of the air, giving it a swirling motion and directing it toward the oil cone beyond the diffuser.
