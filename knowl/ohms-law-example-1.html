Example 4.1.12. Find Current.

Given \(E\) = 24 V and \(R\) = 100 \(\Omega\text{,}\) determine the current \(I\text{.}\)
Answer.
\(I\) = 0.24 A
Solution.
Directly applying (4.1.6) we get:
\begin{equation*} I = \frac{E}{R} = \frac{24 \text{ V}}{100\ \Omega} = 0.24 \text{ A} = 240 \text{ mA}\text{.} \end{equation*}
