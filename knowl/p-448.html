The correct formula for calculating power in a three-phase AC circuit is
\begin{equation} P = \sqrt{3} E I \cos\theta\tag{4.1.5} \end{equation}
where:
\begin{align*} P \amp = \text{Real electrical power (RMS, in watts)}\\ I \amp = \text{Current (RMS, in amperes)}\\ V \amp = \text{Voltage (RMS, in volts)}\\ \theta \amp = \text{phase angle} \end{align*}
